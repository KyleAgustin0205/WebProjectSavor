Who We Are

A Passion Project
Turned Dining Destination

Savor & Co. was born out of a simple belief: great food brings people together. Founded in 2026 by Chef Maria Santos, our kitchen is a celebration of Filipino flavors reimagined with a modern, artisan touch.

Every ingredient is handpicked from trusted local farmers in Pampanga, ensuring freshness and sustainability in every plate we serve.
